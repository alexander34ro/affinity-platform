# CHIML Semantic

* *rootChain*
    - **{ins:** *ins* **, out:** *out* **, vars:** *vars* **, verbose:** *verbosity* **, if:** *condition*, **do:** *command*, **while:** *condition* **}**
* *chain*
    - **{ins:** *ins*, **out:** *out*, **if:** *condition*, **do:** *command*, **while:** *condition* **}**
    - **"(** *ins* **)->** *command* **->** *out* **"**
* *ins*
    - *array*
* *out*
    - *variableName*
* *array*
    - **[]**
    - **[** *elements* **]**
    - **"** *elements* **"**
* *elements*
    - *variableName*
    - *variableName* **,** *elements*
